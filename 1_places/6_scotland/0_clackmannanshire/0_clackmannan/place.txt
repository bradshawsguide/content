Text:

Here is a school, at which M. Bruce the author of "Lochloven" was master. In the vicinity are *Clackmamnan House*, near which are Brace's old tower and church, rebuilt in the pointed style by Gillespie, belonging to the Earl of Zetland, Kennetpans and Kilbaggie distilleries, Devon iron works, and *Brucefield*, the seat of Lord Abercrombie. The county returns one member jointly with Kinross-shire.
