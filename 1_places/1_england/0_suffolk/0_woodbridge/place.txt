Text: This is a little town of some commercial importance, having a considerable coasting trade, besides exporting large quantities of grain. It lies on the banks of the river Deben, about 8 miles from the sea, but which is here navigable for vessels of about 120 tons. The church is a fine spacious building, and has several monuments.
