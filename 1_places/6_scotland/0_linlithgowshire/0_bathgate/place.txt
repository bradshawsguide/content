section: 3
----
title: Bathgate
----
notes:
- A telegraph station.
----
text: Here a corn market is held on Wednesdays, and fairs on the first Wednesday after Whit-Sunday; fourth Wednesday in June and October; third  Wednesday in August; Wednesday after Martinmas, *o.s.* The rains of Walter Stewart's Castle, given to him by Bruce, may still be seen.
