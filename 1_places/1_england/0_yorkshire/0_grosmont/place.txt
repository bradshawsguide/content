Text:

Here the moorland commences. This town contains an old church and the ruins of a castle founded by the Saxon Morear, where Richard II. was kept a prisoner until his removal to Pontefract. Two towers still remain, bearing the names of Queen Elizabeth and Rosamond's. In the vicinity are *Pickering Hall*, seat of Mrs. Dowker; *Pickering Beck*; *Shunor Hoe*, 1,404 feet high; *Ralph's Cross*, 1,485 feet, and *Boltom Head*, 1,485 feet.
