[Thornton](/stations/thornton) station, junction of the Dunfermline Branch.

Passing the intermediate stations of [Cardenden](/stations/cardenden) and [Lochgelly](/stations/lochgelly) we come to [Cowdenbeath](/stations/cowdenbeath), the point of connection with the [Kinross-shire Railway](/routes/cowdenbeath-to-kinross).

[Crossgates](/stations/crossgates) and [Halbeath](/stations/halbeath) stations, at the latter of which there is good coal.
