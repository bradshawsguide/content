text: This is a small market-town, with a population of 3,380, employed in the cotton, linen, sacking, and sail-cloth manufactures. There are remains of an old Norman church. The present edifice was built in 1822. It has a good *Grammar School*. In the vicinity is *Ribby Hall* the seat of H. Hornby, Esq.
