Text: This is a market town, with a good free grammar school, an old church with thatched chancel and ancient tombs, salt springs, and is the head quarters of a Wesleyan Circuit. In the vicinity are *Ormesby*, the seat of Mr. Massingherd; *Willoughby*, the seat of the brave Lord Willoughby; *Well Vale*, R. Dashwood, Esg; and *Skendleby*, Sir E. Brackenbury, Bart.
