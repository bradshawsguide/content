Here the Highlanders were defeated, in 1648. M'Corquodale and Co.'s printing works should be visited. In the vicinity are Castle Hill, with its old oaks, and *Haydock Park*, seat of T. Legh, Esq., used as a private Lunatic Asylum, under the direction of Mr. Sutton.
