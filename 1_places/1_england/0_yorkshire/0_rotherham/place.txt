Text:

Here is a large old church, and the *Independent's College*. Sandstone is quarried here for the cutlers. In the vicinity are *Thundercliffe Grange*, Earl of Effingham; *Aldwark Hall*, *Thrybergh Hall*, and *Ravenfeld*.
