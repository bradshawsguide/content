text: Near which is *Leeswood*, seat of J. W. Egton, Esq., and *Plas Newydd*, the Marquis of Anglesea's; thence we soon reach Llong, near which is a tower, the old three-storied and machicolated seat of the Wynnes, where the Mayor of Chester was murdered by the Welsh Prince Reinallt in 1475.
