Text: A considerable trade in the manufacture of tartan and tweed cloths is here carried on. A tine church, three chapels, ten woollen mills, brewery, large tan yards, library, grammar school; close to which was the Hunting Tower of the Scottish Kings. Close at hand are Mugget Hill, Gala Water, Allan Water (*the Glendeary of the Monastery*), Lauder, Cowdenknowes, with its vitrified fort, and Ashestiel, where Scott wrote his *Marmion*.
