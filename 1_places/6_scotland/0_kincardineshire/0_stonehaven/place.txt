text: Since the reign of James VI. this place has been the county town of Kincardineshire, and contains a population of 3,009, who are principally engaged in the herring fisheries, distilleries, and breweries. It has a market house, town hall, dispensary, bridge over the Cowie which joins the old and new towns, water and gas works, quay, Donaldson's free school, a pier, with two fixed lights, which can be seen at a distance of 13 miles; a harbour, with 16 feet of water; two good churches, and four chapels.
