text: This is a much-frequented watering-place, near Crosby Point, at the mouth of the Mersey. It contains 3,794 inhabitants, an excellent free grammar and girls' school. In the vicinity is *Crosby Hall*, the seat of William Blundell Esq., whose family have held the manor for upwards of seven centuries.
