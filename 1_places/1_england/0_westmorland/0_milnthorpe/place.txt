text: This place is a market town, with a population of 1,433, who are employed in the coasting trade, flax, flour, and paper mills. It contains a fine Gothic church, school, and chapel. In the; vicinity are *Dallam Tower*, seat of George E. Wilson, Esq. *Levens Hall*, Hon. Mrs. Howard:. *Sizergh Castle*, the ancient seat of the Stricklands.
