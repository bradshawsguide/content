title: Routes & Tours
----
title_short: Routes
----
sections:
- uid: 1
  title: Section I
  text: **London and its Environs.** Tours through the counties of Kent, Sussex, Hants, Dorset, Devon, the Channel Islands, and the Isle of Wight.
- uid: 2
  title: Section II
  text: **North & South Wales, Ireland and the Lakes of Killarney.** Tours through the counties of Berks, Buckingham, Wilts, Dorset, Cornwall, Somerset, Gloucester, the South Wales districts, Oxford, Warwick, Salop, Chester, Flint, Carnarvon, Anglesea, and through Ireland.
- uid: 3
  title: Section III
  text: **English & Scotch Lake Districts, Ayr (the Land of Burns).** Tours through the counties of Hertford, Buckingham, Northampton, Warwick, Stafford, Chester, Lancaster, York, Cumberland, Westmorland, Northumberland, Dumfries, Lanark, Ayr, and the Northern Counties of Scotland.
- uid: 4
  title: Section IV
  text: **The Great Manufacturing Districts of Lancashire and Yorkshire.** Tours through the counties of Essex, Suffolk, Norfolk, Hertford, Cambridge, Huntingdon, Rutland, Leicester, Nottingham, Lincoln, Lancaster (South East), York, Durham, Northumberland, Berwick, and the South-Eastern Counties of Scotland.
