Title: Bradford

----

Section: 2

----

Subtitle: Bradford-on-Avon

----

Notes: A telegraph station.

----

Text:

A town that "stondeth by clooth making," said Leland, three centuries ago, and the same may be said of it now. The Avon is crossed by two bridges, one a very ancient one, with a chapel over one of the piers.
