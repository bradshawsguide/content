Title: Wells

----

Section: 4

----

Notes: A telegraph station.

----

Text: A small seaport town, having a spacious harbour, but not easy of access. Population, 3,098. Here is an oyster fishery. Corn and malt are shipped. Coals, timber, deals, bark, &c., &c., imported.
