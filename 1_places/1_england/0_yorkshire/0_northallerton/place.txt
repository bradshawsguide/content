Text: This is a small borough, with a population of 4,755 (who return one member), employed in the linen trade. Here are remains of Friaries founded in 1341 and 1354. It has an old Church; Grammar School, in which Archbishop Palliser, Rymer, Kettlewell, Dean, Hickes, Burnet, and Dr. Radcliffe were educated.
