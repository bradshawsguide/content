text: Here fairs are held on the first Friday after March 11th; first Friday after 4th May; first Tuesday after. June 4th; first Friday after 25th July and August; Wednesday before first Friday in November; and Friday after 25th December.

*Rutherglen Church* is famous on account of two great national transactions; it was here that Edward I. signed the treaty in 1297, and Monteith covenanted to betray Wallace.
