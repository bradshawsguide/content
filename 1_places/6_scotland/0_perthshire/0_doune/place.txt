Title: Doune

----

Section: 3

----

Notes: Telegraph station at Bridge of Allan.

----

Text:

This place is remarkable for its Castle, now in ruins, cresting the top of a lofty eminence, overlooking the Teith. Its noble founder is supposed to be Murdoch, Duke of Albany. It affords a fine opportunity for the rambler.
