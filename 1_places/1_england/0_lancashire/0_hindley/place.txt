title: Hindley
----
notes:
- Distance from station, ½ mile.
- A telegraph station.
----
text: This place has a mining population of 8,477. "Here is, or was, a burning well of cold water, through which rose an inflammable gas, produced by the decomposition of sulphate of iron."—Sharp's <cite>British Gazetteer</cite>. In the vicinity are *Hindley Hall*, Right Hon. Lord Kingsdown; and *Bradshawe Hall*, T. Bradshawe, Esq.
