Text:

This place formerly supplied Government with gun flints. Lord Mayor Eyre, who built Leadenhall Market, was a native.

In the vicinity are *Brandon Park*, seat of E. Bliss, Esq.; *Backenham House*, Lord Petre's; *Merton*, Lord Walsingham's, in the Elizabethan style, &c.
