text: This town was built by the Blaneys in 1611, and has a population of about 3,484, who are principally shop-keepers. Large flour mills are in the vicinity. The Ulster canal passes the town. Its public buildings consist of a county court house and jail, cavalry barracks, fever hospital, and workhouse. Close at hand are the ruins of some forts. *Cortalvin*, Lord Rossmore; *Castle Shane*, Right Hon. E. Lucas; *Camla Vale*, Colonel Westenra.
