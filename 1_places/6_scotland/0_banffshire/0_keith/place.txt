Title: Keith

----

Section: 3

----

Notes: A telegraph station.

----

Text:

A small town of 2,648 inhabitants, consisting only of one long street. The church stands on the site of a very old one, and was rebuilt, in a pointed style, in 1819. The tower is 104 feet high. Ferguson, the astronomer, was born here.
