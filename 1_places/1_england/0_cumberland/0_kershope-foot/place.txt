Title: Kershope Foot

----

Section: 4

----

Text: Situated at the foot of the porphyry range of hills called the Cheviots. The small stream called the Kershope, which here empties itself into the Liddel, runs along their base, and both together taking a north-easterly direction, form another section of the boundary line between Roxburgh and Cumberland.
