Text:

Famous for its cotton mills, belonging to Messrs-Strutt; its hose bleaching mills and nail factories are likewise worth notice. The church is modern and handsome. The ancient chapel is used as a school-room, and is said to have been built by John of Gaunt. *Belper House*, Lord Belper. The timber viaducts over Belper Pool are nearly 500 feet long.
