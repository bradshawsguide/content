Text:

This is a small market town, with a population of 1,749, employed in the mines and quarries. The church contains tombs of the Blenkinsops, of *Bilisten Castle* (opposite the town), and John, Bishop Ridley the martyr's brother. At *Castle Banks*, is a mound, and at *Whitchester*, a Roman camp. *Blenkinsop Hall*, seat of J. Coulson, Esq., and *Unthank Hall*, D. Dixon, Esq., are close at hand.
