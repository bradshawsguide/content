Text:

In the vicinity are *Hatton Castle* in nuns, which was built in 1575, by Lord Oliphant; Castle of *Balcraig* and *Auchtertyre Camp*, both, ruins; at the latter of which Montrose rested. From Kilfarnie Beacon a view of ten counties is obtained, as far as St. Abb's Head, on a clear day.
