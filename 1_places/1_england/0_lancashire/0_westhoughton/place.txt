Title: Westhoughton

----

Section: 3

----

Notes: A telegraph station.

----

Text:

This place has a population of 5,156, who are employed in the muslin trade. In 1812, a dreadful Luddite riot took place, at which a large quantity of machinery was destroyed by the mob.
