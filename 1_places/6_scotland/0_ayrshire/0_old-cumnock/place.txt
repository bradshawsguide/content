Text:

In the vicinity are *Dumfries House*, *Ochiltree*, *Logan House*, and *Terrenzean Castle*, a fine ruin. At Borland, the pretty scat of the Hamiltons, are the ruins of a chapel. Plane-tree snuff-boxes are made here.
