Text:

(smcp: Standish) is the seat of the Standishes, a very ancient family. *Haigh Hall*, the seat of the Earl of Crawford and Balcarres, a descendant of the Bradshaighs, who lived here for many centuries. *Ashton Hall*, a fine seat, is in the district of *Mackerfield*.
