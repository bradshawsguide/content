Text:

This place is situated on the river Ericht, which abounds with fish. It is celebrated for its pure white marble, highly esteemed by sculptors and architects. In the vicinity are Graighall Ratray, from which some fine views may be obtained, *Loch of Clunie*, with its ancient Castle, *Ardblair*, and *Newton House*, the seat of the M'Phersons.
