Title: Newtownards

----

Section: 2

----

Desc: A town of muslin weavers and embroiderers, belonging to the Londonderry family, at the head of Lough Strangford.

----

Text:

A town of muslin weavers and embroiderers, belonging to the Londonderry family, at the head of Lough Strangford, which is a lagoon full of islands, with nothing notable about it except Grey Abbey. "Will Watch, the bold smuggler," flourished at Newtownards.
