title: Sutherlandshire
----
text: Famous for the beauty and sublimity of its scenery, the fertility of its eastern farms, the extent of its pastoral districts, the height of its hills, its splendid lochs, and its vast sea-board.

Coaches twice a day bring the tourist to the coast side districts, in the first of which is the ancient cathedral town of [Dornoch](/places/scotland/sutherlandshire/dornoch).
