Title: Camborne

----

Section: 2

----

Notes: A telegraph station.

----

Text:

A mining town of much importance. The *Dolcoath* mine is one of the deepest in the country, being 2,000 feet below the surface. In the vicinity are *Pendarves*, *Clowance*, and *Tehidy Park*, Lady Bassett.
