section: 3
----
title: Kintore
----
notes:
- Telegraph station at Inverury, 3 miles.
----
text: This was formerly a forest, and had a hunting seat given by Bruce to the Keiths. In the vicinity are *Thainston*, seat of D. Mitchell. Esq., Castle-hill Law, Bruce's Howe, some Cairns and the remains of four Druid circles.
