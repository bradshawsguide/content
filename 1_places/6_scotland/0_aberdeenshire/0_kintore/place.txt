Title: Kintore

----

Section: 3

----

Notes: Telegraph station at Inverury, 3 miles.

----

Text:

This was formerly a forest, and had a hunting seat given by Bruce to the Keiths. In the vicinity are *Thainston*, seat of D. Mitchell. Esq., Castle-hill Law, Bruce's Howe, some Cairns and the remains of four Druid circles.
