Title: Formby and Altcar

----

Section: 3

----

Notes: A telegraph station.

----

Text:

This bathing-place was once a market-town. It is situated near Formby Point, in the Irish Sea, and outside the bans there is a floating light. It is much frequented by the Lancastrians. Population, 1,780.
