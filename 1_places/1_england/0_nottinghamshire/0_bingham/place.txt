Text: Situated near the Fosseway, is a small market town in the vale of Belroir, with a good early Gothic church; close to which are *Shelford*, a seat of the Stanhopes, and *Wiverton*, an old deserted house of the Chaworths, both of which were on the royalist side in the civil war, and made work for Hutchinson in au occasional skirmish.
