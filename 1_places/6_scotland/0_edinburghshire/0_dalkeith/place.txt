----

Text: This burgh, is beautifully situated on the Esk river. *Dalkeith Palace*, the seat of the Duke of Baccleugh, was built by Ann, Duchess of Monmouth, on the site of the old castle, visited by Froissart, James VI., Charles I., who was a prisoner here, General Monk, who took it, George IV. in 1822, and Queen Victoria in 1848.
