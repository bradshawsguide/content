Text: Here is a large old church. In the vicinity are *Hunsley Beacon*, 530 feet high; *Houghton Hall*, the seat of the Hon. C. Langdale; *Goodmanham*, with its Norman church, built on the site of a Pagan Grove, which was burnt by Crifin in the 7th century, on his conversion by Paulinus, and Lord Hotham's seat, *South Dalton Hall*, where bones of the elephant and rhinoceros have been found.
