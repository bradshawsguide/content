Here Bruce defeated the Earl of Pembroke. Close at hand is *Loudon Castle*, seat of the Marquis of Hastings, *Burr Castle*, at which are many cairns, and an elm 24 feet in girth, and *Wallace Hill*, the retreat of that gallant hero, and where he defeated Fenwick.
