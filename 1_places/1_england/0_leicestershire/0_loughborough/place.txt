Text: This town, with a population of 10,830, is a seat of the lace and stocking trades. In 1557 it was depopulated by the sweating sickness. In the vicinity are *Prestwold Hall*, with its fine collection of pictures, seat of C. Packe, Esq., M P.; *Garendon Park*, C. Philips, Esq; and *Walton House*, E. Dawson, Esq.
