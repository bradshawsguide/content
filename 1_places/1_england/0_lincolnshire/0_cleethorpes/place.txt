Title: Cleethorpes

----

Section: 4

----

Text: A fine watering place about two miles below Grimsby. It is prettily situated at the mouth of the Humber, on a flat sandy beach. *Spurn Head*, with its light houses, is on the opposite side of the Humber, which is here about seven miles wide.
