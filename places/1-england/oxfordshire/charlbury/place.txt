text: *Ditchley* (2 miles), Lord Dillon, was the birthplace of the notorious Lord Rochester. Near it is *Kiddington*, with an old church, of which Wharton was rector. 6 miles distant is *Heythorpe*, another seat of the Duke of Beaufort. *Glympton*, on the river Gline, E. Way, Esq. From this station *Wychwood Forest*, (a fine wooded track of much sylvan beauty) soon to be reclaimed, may be visited. Warton the poet has immortalised the scenery about here.
