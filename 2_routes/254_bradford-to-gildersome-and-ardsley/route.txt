(place: england/yorkshire/bradford)

This is a short line from Bradford to Gildersome and Ardsley. It passes through the stations Of [Laister Dyke](/stations/laisterdyke), [Dudley Hill](/stations/dudley-hill), [Birkenshaw](/stations/birkenshaw), to [Drighlington](/stations/drighlington), close to which is

(place: england/yorkshire/adwalton)

(branch: start)
  At Drighlington another line turns off to the right, passing the stations of [Upper Batley](/stations/upper-batley), [Batley](/stations/batley), [Osset](/stations/osset), and [Flushdyke](/stations/flushdyke), to the Westgate station at Wakefield, and from thence to the Lancashire and Yorkshire station at bottom of Kirkgate.
(branch: end)

The Gildersome line from Drighlington passes through a district rich in mineral products, through [Gildersome](/stations/gildersome), [Morley](/stations/morley), and [Tingley](/stations/tingley), to [Ardsley](/stations/ardsley), the junction of the Bradford, Wakefield, and Leeds line, and from thence *via* [Lofthouse](/stations/lofthouse) to

(place: england/yorkshire/wakefield)
