Text:

Brampton is a curious old-fashioned place, with 2,379 inhabitants, engaged in the check and gingham factories. Close at hand is *Naworth Castle*, seat of Lord Carlisle. It was occupied by the Pretender in 1745. Here is a *grammar school*, erected on the site of an hospital, founded in 1688. Below, in Mary's Holme, are the gate and towers of *Lanercost Abbey*, as old as the 12th century. *Walton House*, seat or W. Johnson, Esq., Castlesteads, *Irthingtor Cross* and *Watchcross*.
