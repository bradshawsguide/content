section: 3
----
title: Alford
----
text: A place remarkable for the defeat of the Covenanters by Montrose in 1645. It is situated on the Don, in which are salmon fisheries, and has a fair once a month for cattle. In the vicinity are *Haughton House*, J. Farquharson, Esq., *Castle Forbes*, Lord Forbes, and *Whitehaugh*, -- Forbes, Esq.
